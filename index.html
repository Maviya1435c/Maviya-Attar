Solapur is a city located in the southwestern region of the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is known for its rich history, textile industry, and religious sites.

  • History: Solapur has a long and interesting history, dating back to the 2nd century BC. The city has been ruled by various dynasties, including the Mauryas, the Chalukyas, and the Marathas.
  • Textile industry: Solapur is a major center for the textile industry in India. The city is famous for its production of chadars (bedsheets) and towels.
  • Religious sites: Solapur is home to a number of important religious sites, including the Siddheshwar Temple, the Akkalkot Swami Temple, and the Pandharpur Temple.
  • Gadda Yatra: Solapur is also known for its annual Gadda Yatra, a nine-day religious festival that is celebrated with great pomp and show.
